Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What is the goal of Excursionist?

    A: Excursionist provides a consolidated platform for users to be able to organize and plan their travel experiences. You can use Excursionist to create an itinerary with a brief description and location tag.

  • Q: Why is my map not loading, but my itineraries still work?

    A: The map integrated into Excursionist is ran via API and thus, may have it's own issues aside from Excursionist. Please determine the root of the error before trying to address the cause.
